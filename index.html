A reputation built on

At Ego, our mission has always been to bring scientific rigour to skincare. It’s what’s helped us earn an international reputation for delivering effective, high quality and innovative products to manage skin conditions and maintain healthy skin.

Excellence in the quality of everything we do is inherent in our culture. We are always striving to improve our systems, processes and products. That’s why we invested $75-million – more than half of our sales revenue – in research and development last year.

Strong ethics are also central to our philosophy, particularly with regard to our relationships with health professionals, institutions, ' government, our customers and the communities in each of the countries we operate.
